Security architecture

Security is the foundation.

Relay’s roadmap begins with secure configuration and identity before networking or terminal access. The goal is simple: your credentials should remain protected and under your control.

01

02

03

04

Strict host verification

05

Biometric approval

06

Relay is currently in early development. These security capabilities are planned and documented in the public roadmap; they are not all available in the current build.
